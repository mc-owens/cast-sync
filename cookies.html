Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how CastSync uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, and to provide information to the site owner.

2. How We Use Cookies

CastSync uses cookies only for the purpose of keeping you logged in. Specifically:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
connect.sid Essential Maintains your login session so you don't have to sign in on every page. Session (deleted when you close your browser, or after 7 days of inactivity)

We do not use cookies for advertising, cross-site tracking, analytics, or any purpose other than keeping you logged in.

3. Third-Party Cookies

When you complete a payment, you will be redirected to Stripe's hosted checkout page. Stripe may set its own cookies during that process. We do not control those cookies. You can review Stripe's cookie practices in their Privacy Policy.

If you sign in with Google, Google may set cookies as part of the authentication process. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions vary by browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling or deleting the session cookie will sign you out of CastSync and you will need to log in again.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

6. Contact

Questions about our cookie use? Contact us at support@cast-sync.com or visit our Contact page.
