How it works

From audition to cast list in three steps

No setup fees, no complex software. Directors are up and running in minutes.

1
Share your production code

Create a production and share the 6-character code with your auditionees. They create a free account in seconds, then submit their info, availability, and known conflicts.

2
Build your schedule and cast

See everyone's availability at a glance. Add pieces, assign rehearsal blocks, and cast dancers — CastSync flags conflicts automatically so you don't double-book anyone.

Send the full cast list to every auditionee with one click. They see their role, their piece's rehearsal schedule, and any message you choose to include.

FAQ

Common questions

Do auditionees need to create an account? +
Yes — they sign up with an email and password, then enter your production code to submit. Their profile saves automatically so they only fill in personal info once across all future auditions.
What is a "production"? +
A production is one audition cycle — a Fall Showcase, Spring Concert, or Workshop, for example. Each production has its own join code, auditionee list, pieces, and cast. You can run as many as you need under one organization.
How does co-director access work? +
You can invite a co-director to a specific production by email. They get full access to that show's data — scheduling, casting, and publishing — without seeing your other productions.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Sessions are encrypted, passwords are hashed with bcrypt, and we never share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
